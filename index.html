chicken karahi recipe

chicken karahi pakistan mei bhut pasand ki jati ha our iski kuch waja ha jo is logon ka favorite bana deti ha

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2table spoon oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1table spoon ginger and garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2-3 chilles (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • 3/2 cup water ( adjust for gravy)

method

  1. Heat oil in a large karahi over medium heat
  2. add chopped oinions and saute until golden brown
  3. Add chicken pieces to the karahi and cook until they are browend on all sides
  4. Add chopped tomatoes ,green chilies, redchili poweder,turmeric, and salt stir well and cook until the tomatoes soften
  5. Add water and let thechiken cook for 20-25 minutes or tender and the oil separates from the gravy
  6. Sprinkle garam masla and kasuri mehti over the dish stir to mix
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice
